Pen & Stage

Where every story finds its spotlight.

Expression Unbound

Pen and Stage is a creative sanctuary for writers, actors, and poets. We believe that every voice deserves to be heard, whether it’s through the quiet stroke of a pen or a powerful monologue under the theater lights.

Creative Showcases

From open mic nights to full-scale dramatic productions, we provide the platform for students to present their vulnerability and vision to the community.

The club invites guest speakers—authors, playwrights, and theater professionals—to share real-world insights, inspiring members to pursue their passions with professional purpose.

Joining Pen and Stage means becoming part of a creative family. Members develop critical life skills: public speaking, teamwork, empathy, and leadership. Here, you don’t just learn theater; you learn to connect.

"Every story matters. Every voice deserves a spotlight."
