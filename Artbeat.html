Creativity

From traditional oil painting to modern digital illustration, the Artbeat club provides the tools and the space for students to experiment with different mediums. We regularly host "Canvas Nights" and design competitions to push our boundaries.

Our club also collaborates with the Binary Club to work on UI/UX elements, ensuring that our technical projects are as beautiful as they are functional. At Artbeat, we don't just make art—we make an impact.
