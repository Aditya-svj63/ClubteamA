Logic-Driven

Members work together on group projects, participate in lively debates, and solve complex problems through trial and error. From constructing physical models to conducting chemistry experiments, we focus on learning by doing.

By linking classroom theory with practical experience, the club helps students see the impact of science on everyday life. Whether you dream of being an engineer or just love asking "why," there is a place for you here.
