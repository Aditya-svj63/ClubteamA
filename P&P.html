Our Commitment

Focused on sustainability and social responsibility, this club inspires students to protect the planet and serve communities through meaningful action, awareness campaigns, and leadership initiatives.

Members take on roles as project leads, event coordinators, and sustainability ambassadors, gaining valuable experience in teamwork and public speaking. A digital Sustainability Scoreboard tracks the club’s impact—trees planted, waste reduced, and volunteer hours logged.

The club’s reach extends beyond the school, collaborating with local municipal departments to scale initiatives. Annual events like the Eco Fest bring the entire school community together for a celebration of sustainability, featuring workshops, stalls, and guest speakers.

"Planet and People is a space where passion meets purpose. It welcomes anyone curious about the environment or looking for a community of changemakers."
